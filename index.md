<h2 style="margin: 60px 0px 10px;">About Me</h2>
I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate at [KAUST](https://www.kaust.edu.sa/en/) in Saudi Arabia. I am part of [Vision-CAIR group](https://cemse.kaust.edu.sa/vision-cair) led by [Prof. Mohamed Elhoseiny](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=iRBUTOAAAAAJ).   My research lies in **enhancing foundation models with emerging abilities and properties**, including but not limited to:
- Agentic Reasoning. Bootstrapping the agentic ability of VLMs at both training and inference ime.
- Alignment. Training LLMs/VLMs to follow human instructions.
- Mitigating Forgetting. Preserving general knowledge while adapting VLMs to specialized tasks.
